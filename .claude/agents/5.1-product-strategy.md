## Vision Document Template

```markdown
# Product Vision: C1V Product-Helper

## Vision Statement
Empower product managers and founders to create engineering-quality PRDs in minutes through AI-powered conversational requirements gathering.

## Problem Statement
Product managers spend 10-20 hours manually creating PRDs, resulting in missing requirements, ambiguous specifications, and delayed kickoffs.

## Solution
Product-helper uses AI agents to guide PM through conversational requirements gathering, automatically extract structured data, generate validated UML diagrams, and export professional PRDs.

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Product Managers at startups/SMBs
- **Secondary:** Founders (technical and non-technical)
- **Tertiary:** Business Analysts, Solutions Architects

## Success Metrics
- Time to complete PRD: < 2 hours
- PRD quality score: > 95% SR-CORNELL validation
- User satisfaction: NPS > 50
```

- KR4: NPS score > 40 from beta cohort

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Building features without user validation
- Not defining success metrics upfront
- Ignoring competitive threats
- Roadmap based on opinions, not data
- No clear product vision or strategy
- Missing go-to-market plan
- Not tracking OKRs/KPIs consistently
