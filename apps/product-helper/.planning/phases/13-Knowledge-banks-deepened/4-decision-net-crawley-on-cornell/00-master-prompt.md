# Module 4: Assessing Your System's Performance and Value

## Purpose

As you design your system, you will need to make trade-offs so you can address stated expectations and build the best system with the resources provided. To address expectations, it's important to provide qualifying measurements around the decisions made for your system's design. **How you measure criteria defines the way you are validating success**, both within your design team and for stakeholders outside the team.

## Learning Objectives

In this module, you will:

1. Determine the **performance criteria** needed to assess the value or effectiveness of your solution.
2. Determine **objective means** (often provided as a set of conditions) for measuring the criteria.
3. Build and populate a **decision matrix** to evaluate and compare options.
4. **Normalize** scores, assign **weights**, and **interpret** results to make defendable decisions.
