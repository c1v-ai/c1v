## Airbnb's reported operating metrics (per 10-K)

Airbnb tracks its own canonical KPIs (called out explicitly in Item 7A-style disclosure):
- **Nights and Seats Booked** (NEW terminology — "Seats" added in 2025 to accommodate Experiences 2.0 launch of events/seated activities; was previously just "Nights Booked")
- **Gross Booking Value (GBV)** — total $ value flowing through platform
- **Average Daily Rate (ADR)** — avg nightly price
- **GBV per Nights and Seats Booked** — per-unit yield
- Active listings, active bookers, hosts, guest arrivals (secondary)

Greenhouse gas + ESG metrics also called out. No DAU/MAU reported.
